About Us

AIM INFOTECH is registered from Ministry of Corporate Affair, Society Act 2012. We are providing computer and vocational courses all over India with the help of our Branch network in small towns and villages.

AIM INFOTECH has also registered under Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises, Govt. of India. AIM INFOTECH is an ISO 9001-2015 Certified institute.

We believe in practical and job oriented courses and provide certifications to the students along with their school and college study so that he/she can get a job or start his/her own business.
